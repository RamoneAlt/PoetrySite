Eamon and Grammy's April Poems

Welcome to a page of peoms written by both Grammy and Eamon back and fourth over the entire month of April in 2023, also known as national poetry month.

Although not specified on each poem, Grammy's poem is on the left side and Eamon's is strictly on the right side.

April 1st

March Off

And lo! it's gone;

another month appears

with showers

(like the saying foretold)

fly by

or sit in trees

looking down at us

who look up at them,

wishing we could fly as they do-

oblivious to the brooding sky

and falling rain.

But alas!

We forgot to grow our wings.

A Young Boy Sitting and Thinking Deeply About Birds on a Rainy Spring Day

Pitter-pat, pitter-pat,

The rain goes by just like that.

Running down the window with little drips,

We mustn’t go outside, so I’ll try to think.

Warm spring heat, the sun high in the day.

The birds chirp with so much to say,

How do they spend their time all day?

Eating worms, flying too.

Playing games, thinking of food?

Makes you think why we’re all at school.

We humans, sit here and argue this and that.

Snaps me back into listening to the pitter-pat.

Pitter-pat, pitter-pat…
